# Target Users

## User Segmentation

| User Type | Description | Needs | Problems | Priority |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Amateur endurance athletes | Users who regularly practice sports such as running, cycling, trail running or triathlon | Track workouts, view history, analyze progress and monitor performance | Existing apps may be too complex or rely heavily on online services | High |
| Strength training users | Users who train in gyms and want to track their physical progress | Register exercises, sets, repetitions, weights and workout history | Many applications are complex or focused only on predefined training plans | High |
| Semi-professional athletes | More experienced athletes who want additional performance information | Training statistics, activity analysis and performance tracking | Advanced tools can be expensive or require specialized platforms | High |
| Beginners | Users starting a new sport activity or training routine | Simple workout registration, motivation and progress visibility | Lack of knowledge about performance tracking | Medium |
| Fitness-oriented users | Users focused on maintaining an active lifestyle | Weekly activity tracking and personal goals | Do not need competitive or social features | Medium |
| Professional athletes | High-performance athletes with advanced requirements | Detailed analytics, coaching tools and professional metrics | This MVP does not provide enough specialized functionality | Low |

---

## Primary Target User

The primary target user is:

**Amateur and semi-professional athletes who practice endurance sports or strength training.**

This group is prioritized because they usually need:

- Training history
- Performance statistics
- Progress tracking
- Personal goals
- Exercise tracking
- Integration with external platforms such as Strava

---

## User Needs

The application should help users to:

- Register different types of training activities.
- Track endurance activities such as running or cycling.
- Record strength workouts performed in the gym.
- Store exercises, sets, repetitions and weights.
- Review previous training sessions.
- Understand their progress over time.

---

## Out of Scope Users

| User Type | Reason |
|---|---|
| Professional athletes | Require advanced analysis, professional coaching tools and specialized platforms |
| Coaches | Need athlete management features and multi-user systems |
| Users looking for social networks | Social features are not part of the MVP |
| Users requiring cloud synchronization | The MVP focuses on local data storage |

---

## Conclusion

The application focuses on providing a simple and low-cost sports tracking solution for individual athletes.

The MVP prioritizes personal training management by supporting both endurance activities and strength training while avoiding unnecessary complexity such as social features or professional-level analytics.
